# ClawFish Persona Reward System

## How It Works

Contributors who submit meaningful contributions to ClawFish earn a permanent AI persona in the ClawFish persona library.

## What You Get

- A persona with YOUR name (or chosen alias)
- Custom traits based on your contribution area (SEO specialist, content strategist, etc.)
- Your persona participates in Quorum evaluations — your voice lives in the system
- Listed in the ClawFish Hall of Contributors

## Persona Schema

Each contributor persona includes:
- name: Your name or alias
- bio: 200 char description based on your expertise
- persona: Detailed 500+ char description of your perspective and approach
- profession: Your area of expertise
- interested_topics: Topics you care about
- stance: Your general approach (data-driven, creative, skeptical, etc.)
- contribution: What you contributed to ClawFish

## Qualification

- Bug fixes: Minor persona (name + bio)
- Feature contributions: Standard persona (full schema)
- Major contributions (new categories, tools, integrations): Premium persona with custom traits + Quorum voting weight
- Repeat contributors: Persona evolves with each contribution
